Braian Plaku (aka swimmingbrain)

About

braian@swimmingbrain:~/about$ cat about.txt

Braian Plaku is a software developer at Julius Blum GmbH in Vorarlberg, Austria — one of the world's leading manufacturers of furniture fittings. Based in Dornbirn, he specializes in computer vision and machine learning, with a focus on 6D pose estimation.

Alongside his work in industry, he is an open-source software developer: under the handle swimmingbrain he builds and publishes projects on GitHub, from machine-learning experiments to web apps and games. He is completing a BSc in Computer Science (Digital Innovation) at FH Vorarlberg (FHV) and an MSc in Artificial Intelligence at Johannes Kepler University Linz (JKU), having previously earned a BSc in Electrical Engineering.
