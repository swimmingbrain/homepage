Welcome to WoGsi?!

Test your knowledge of Vorarlberg's beautiful locations. You'll be shown a street view or satellite image, and you need to guess where it is on the map.

How to Play:

  • Look around the panorama view using your mouse
  • Click on the map to place your guess
  • The closer you are, the more points you get!
  • Maximum 5000 points per round
Checking Mapillary connection...

Setup Required

To play this game, you need a free Mapillary API key. Don't worry, it's quick and free!

Quick Setup (2 minutes):

  1. Visit Mapillary Developers
  2. Sign up/Login (you can use Facebook or email)
  3. Create a new application (name it anything)
  4. Copy your Client Token
  5. Paste it below

Your key is saved locally and never shared.
